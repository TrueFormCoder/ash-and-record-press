## Clean Repair Doctrine

A case shows clean repair when contradiction is metabolized without requiring denial, humiliation theater, ghost dependence, or premature substitute closure.

### 7 Clean Repair Markers
1. Claim Separation Improved
2. Truth Remains Public
3. Revision Does Not Require Ritual Humiliation
4. Substitute Installation Slows
5. Access Flattens
6. Residue Is Named Rather Than Denied
