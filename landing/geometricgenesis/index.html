Definition

Geometric Genesis is the architectural layer within ELEOS that governs how behavioral structures form, differentiate, and evolve. It provides the formation logic for archetype emergence, role crystallization, and state-space organization.

What it does

Maps how behavioral patterns come into being. Tracks their structural evolution from latent potential to active expression. Governs the geometry of state transitions, role assignments, and system-level organization across domains.
